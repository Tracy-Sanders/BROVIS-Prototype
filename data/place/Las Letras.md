Las Letras, known as the Literary Quarter, is a vibrant neighborhood in Madrid that celebrates Spanish literary heritage. This intimate district features narrow, car-limited streets lined with the former homes of Golden Age icons, most notably Miguel de Cervantes, author of *Don Quixote*. The neighborhood remains a haven for book lovers, with independent bookshops, literary cafés, and cultural institutions throughout its charming streets. The pedestrian-friendly atmosphere and rich historical significance make it essential for anyone interested in Spanish literature and history.
