Chiang Mai is Thailand's northern cultural capital, renowned for its temples, traditional crafts, and rich heritage. The city serves as a key hub in comprehensive travel itineraries that span Thailand's major destinations, typically paired with Bangkok and Phuket in 12-day packages showcasing everything from urban centers to island paradises. Visitors can explore ancient temples, night markets, and the surrounding mountains while experiencing authentic northern Thai culture.
