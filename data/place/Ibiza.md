Ibiza is a premier destination in Spain's [[Balearic Islands]], attracting approximately seven million tourists annually (verified 2026-04-16). The island is famous for its legendary nightclub scene where top international DJs perform during summer months, making it the ultimate clubbing destination for electronic music enthusiasts worldwide. Beyond the party scene, Ibiza offers pristine beaches, remote coves like [[Cala Comte]] and [[Cala Vadella]], the UNESCO-listed old town of [[Dalt Villa]], and a laid-back boho-glam lifestyle that blends high-energy nightlife with serene beach experiences and local cultural attractions.
