Pão de Açúcar (Sugarloaf Mountain) is one of Rio de Janeiro's most famous landmarks, accessible by cable car and offering spectacular views of the city, beaches, and mountains. The site provides one of the most iconic vantage points for experiencing Rio's natural beauty and urban landscape. Best visited during daylight hours for photography and clear sightlines across the city, surrounding beaches, and the Atlantic Ocean. The cable car system operates regularly (verified 2026-04-16), making it one of Rio's most accessible major attractions.
