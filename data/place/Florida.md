Florida is a southeastern U.S. state renowned for its tropical climate, pristine beaches, and diverse attractions. The state serves as home to major cities like [[Miami]], which functions as a cultural and economic hub with significant international connections. Beyond Miami, Florida offers varied experiences ranging from theme parks in [[Orlando]] to natural ecosystems and coastal destinations. The state's warm weather and accessible beaches make it a premier destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.
