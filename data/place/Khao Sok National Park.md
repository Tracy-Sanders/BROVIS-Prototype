Khao Sok National Park is one of Southern Thailand's most immersive natural attractions, renowned for its night safari tours that reveal the park's hidden nocturnal wildlife and vibrant rainforest ecosystem. Located in the heart of the jungle, it offers a striking contrast to the beach-focused attractions of nearby [[Phuket]], providing adventurers with an authentic rainforest experience. The park is easily accessible as part of multi-day island-hopping adventures from [[Phuket]] and [[Krabi]], making it a popular addition to Southern Thailand itineraries. Best visited for nature exploration, wildlife observation, and jungle adventure activities.
