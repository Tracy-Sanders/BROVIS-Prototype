The Catedral de Caracas stands at the heart of [[Plaza Bolivar]] and represents one of Venezuela's most significant religious and architectural monuments. The cathedral is renowned for housing a notable painting by Peter Paul Rubens depicting Christ's resurrection, a masterpiece that draws both pilgrims and art enthusiasts. Its colonial architecture and spiritual importance make it an essential stop for understanding Caracas's cultural heritage and religious history.
