El Hatillo is one of the safer and more upscale areas of Caracas, located in the eastern section of the city. The neighborhood offers excellent shopping, dining, and other amenities where tourists can feel more comfortable exploring on foot. Known for its commercial establishments and relatively secure environment, El Hatillo serves as a pleasant base for visitors seeking a more comfortable urban experience in the capital.
