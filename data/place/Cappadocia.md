Cappadocia is a historically rich region in central [[Türkiye]] celebrated for its distinctive fairy-tale rock formations, underground cities, and Byzantine heritage. Featured prominently in the Treasures of Türkiye tour departing from [[Istanbul]], it represents a major cultural and historical destination showcasing Anatolian adventures. The region's unique landscape combines volcanic geology with millennia of human settlement, making it essential for travelers interested in history and culture. Cappadocia offers an immersive experience into Turkey's diverse heritage alongside nearby destinations like [[Ephesus]] and [[Izmir]].
