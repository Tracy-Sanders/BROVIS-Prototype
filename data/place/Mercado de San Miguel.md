Mercado de San Miguel is a premier culinary destination in Madrid's historic city center, housed in a beautifully restored 19th-century iron structure. Originally opened as a wholesale food market over 100 years ago, it has evolved into one of Spain's most vibrant dining experiences, featuring more than 20 stands serving authentic Spanish cuisine. Visitors can sample everything from Iberian ham and fresh seafood to traditional tapas, making it an essential stop for food lovers exploring [[Madrid]]. The market represents a key intersection of Spanish culinary tradition and modern gastronomy, offering both casual bites and curated tasting experiences in an atmospheric, bustling environment.
