Spain is a major European destination featuring diverse regions and attractions across the Iberian Peninsula. The country is renowned for its flamenco culture, world-class tapas cuisine, historic architecture spanning from medieval castles to modernist masterpieces, and vibrant nightlife. Spain's Mediterranean and Atlantic coastlines offer pristine beaches, while its interior cities showcase centuries of artistic and cultural heritage.

The country encompasses major cultural hubs including [[Barcelona]] and [[Madrid]], historic cities like [[Seville]], [[Granada]], and [[Málaga]], and culinary capitals such as [[Bilbao]] and [[San Sebastián]]. The [[Balearic Islands]], particularly [[Ibiza]] and [[Mallorca]], are premier Mediterranean destinations known for beaches and nightlife. Spain's diverse regions—from Catalonia to Andalusia to the Basque Country—each offer distinct traditions, cuisines, and landscapes, making it ideal for travelers interested in culture, history, beaches, food, and architecture.
