Royal National Park is a protected natural area located just south of Sydney, offering some of the region's most spectacular coastal scenery and walking trails. The park is renowned for [[Burning Palms Beach]] and features dramatic cliffs, secluded coves, and pristine beaches ideal for swimming and photography. It's one of the best day trip destinations from [[Sydney]], accessible within an hour's drive, making it perfect for coastal walks and nature exploration without venturing far from the city.
