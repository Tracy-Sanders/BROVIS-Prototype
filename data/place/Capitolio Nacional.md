The Capitolio Nacional is the seat of Congress in Venezuela, located at Plaza Bolivar in central Caracas. The building is distinguished by its iconic gold dome and features a monumental ceiling mural depicting the Battle of Carabobo, a pivotal moment in Venezuelan independence history. Essential for understanding Venezuelan politics and architecture, the Capitolio represents the nation's democratic institutions and colonial-era grandeur.
