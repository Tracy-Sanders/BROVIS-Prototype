Las Austrias is the historic heart of Madrid, home to palaces, churches, and monasteries dating back to the 16th century. This neighborhood encompasses some of the city's most significant landmarks, including [[Plaza Mayor]], the [[Royal Palace of Madrid]], and [[Corral de la Moreria]]. It represents the oldest and most historically important area of Madrid, making it essential for anyone interested in Spanish history and architecture. The neighborhood's narrow medieval streets and Renaissance buildings offer an immersive glimpse into Madrid's golden age during the Habsburg dynasty.
