Normandy is a region in northern France celebrated for its profound historical significance, medieval architecture, and picturesque coastal landscapes. Home to iconic sites like [[Mont St-Michel]], the region attracts visitors worldwide seeking to explore its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. The area features numerous medieval landmarks and charming seaside towns that showcase centuries of European history and architectural tradition.
