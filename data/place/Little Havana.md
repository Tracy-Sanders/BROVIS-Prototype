Little Havana is Miami's vibrant Cuban neighborhood, representing an important cultural hub within the Cuban-American community. The area is famous for its authentic Cuban food, sizzling cuisine, and Cuban sandwich shops that reflect the city's strong Cuban influences. Visitors can experience genuine Cuban traditions, street art, and cultural atmosphere that showcase Miami's deep historical roots in Cuban heritage. This neighborhood offers an authentic dining experience and serves as a living testament to the Cuban-American experience in South Florida.
