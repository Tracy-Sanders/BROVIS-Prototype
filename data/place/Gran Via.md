Gran Via is a nearly mile-long shopping street in Madrid that seamlessly blends world-class retail with notable architectural landmarks. The street features an impressive mix of luxury boutiques and mainstream brands, including H&M, the Real Madrid Official Store, the Atletico Madrid Official Store, and Europe's largest Huawei store. Beyond shopping, Gran Via showcases some of Madrid's most distinctive architectural gems, making it essential for visitors interested in both contemporary retail culture and urban design.
