London serves as the primary transport hub for visitors traveling to Stonehenge. Trains depart from London Waterloo to Salisbury station (£25, 1.5 hours, every half hour) (verified 2026-04-15), from which visitors can access Stonehenge via tour buses. The city's extensive rail network makes it an essential starting point for exploring prehistoric monuments in southern England.
