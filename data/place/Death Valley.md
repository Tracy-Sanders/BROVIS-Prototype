Death Valley is a major day trip destination from Las Vegas, located in eastern California. It features epic desert landscapes and is known for its dramatic natural scenery, extreme terrain, and outdoor adventure opportunities. The area is popular for hiking and experiencing some of the most extreme desert environments in North America, making it ideal for those seeking natural wonders and desert exploration.
