Tripea is a fusion restaurant opened in 2017 by Peruvian chef Roberto Martínez Foronda, located in [[Madrid]], Spain. The restaurant combines Peruvian and Southeast Asian ingredients and flavors into an innovative eight-course tasting menu priced at just over $53 per person. Chef Martínez Foronda has successfully navigated fusion cuisine trends to create excellent, distinctive dishes that showcase Madrid's diverse and innovative food scene. Perfect for travelers seeking cutting-edge culinary experiences blending multiple global traditions.
