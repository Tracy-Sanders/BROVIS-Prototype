Bayfront Park is a prominent public park in [[Downtown Miami]] featuring fountains, an amphitheater, and scenic views of [[Biscayne Bay]]. One of Miami's 14 best city parks, it offers free attractions and outdoor recreational spaces with palm trees and waterfront access. The park serves as a notable landmark in downtown [[Miami]], making it an ideal destination for visitors seeking free activities and urban green space.
