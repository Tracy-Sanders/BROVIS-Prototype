The Cycladic Islands are a popular destination for day trips and multi-day excursions from [[Athens]]. This island archipelago is renowned for its saturated blue waters, white-washed villages, and dramatic sunsets. Visitors can cruise on catamarans to experience the region's natural beauty and island-hop between multiple destinations. The islands are featured in Lonely Planet's 'Dreams of Greece' 12-day itinerary, with highlights including the Portara gate in [[Naxos]] and the pristine beaches of [[Galazia Nera]].

Best for beaches, island hopping, sailing, and experiencing quintessential Greek island scenery.
