Copacabana is one of Rio de Janeiro's most iconic beach neighborhoods, famous for its golden sands and vibrant atmosphere. The area is connected to [[Ipanema]] via the Ciclovia Tim Maia bike path and offers numerous dining options, including The Horse's Neck restaurant near posto 6. The neighborhood features active nightlife and cultural attractions, making it a premier destination for beaches, dining, and evening entertainment.

Best for: beaches, nightlife, dining, walking
