Madrid-Barajas Airport is located in northeast Madrid and serves as Spain's primary international gateway. The airport services most major airlines with direct flights to major U.S. cities including New York, Los Angeles, and Miami. As Madrid's main airport, it handles the majority of the city's international air traffic and is well-connected by public transportation to the city center (verified 2026-04-15).
