Naxos is one of the [[Cycladic Islands]] in Greece, easily accessible from [[Athens]] via catamaran. The island's most iconic landmark is the Portara gate, an ancient marble structure that frames ethereal sunset views and serves as a photographer's paradise. Naxos is a popular stop on multi-day island-hopping tours from Athens, particularly featured in the 'Dreams of Greece' 12-day itinerary, making it ideal for travelers seeking beaches, cultural landmarks, and quintessential Cycladic charm.
