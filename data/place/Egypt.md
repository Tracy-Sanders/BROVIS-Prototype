Egypt is a North African country renowned for its ancient historical significance and archaeological treasures. In the 2nd century B.C., Egypt gifted an ancient temple to Spain, which was carefully relocated to Madrid and rebuilt stone by stone, opening as a tourist site in 1972 (verified 2026-04-15). The country remains a premier destination for history and archaeology enthusiasts exploring one of humanity's greatest civilizations.
