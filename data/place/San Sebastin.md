San Sebastián is a stunning coastal city in the [[Basque Country]] region of northern [[Spain]], celebrated as one of Europe's gastronomic capitals. The city's distinct Basque culture, world-class food scene, and beautiful beaches make it an essential destination for travelers exploring the region. San Sebastián features prominently in the 'Basque Country at Its Best' 8-day tour package alongside [[Bilbao]] and the [[Rioja]] wine region, offering visitors an immersive experience of Basque traditions, cuisine, and landscapes.
