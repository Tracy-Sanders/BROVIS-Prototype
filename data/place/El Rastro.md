El Rastro is an iconic open-air market located on Calle de la Ribera de Curtidores in Madrid, operating continuously since 1740 (verified 2026-04-15). Every Sunday and public holiday, the market fills with vendors selling everything from antiques and vintage goods to clothing, furniture, and curiosities. While the crowds can be substantial, numerous shops line the surrounding streets offering quieter shopping alternatives and respite from the bustling stalls.
