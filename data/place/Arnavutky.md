Arnavutköy is a picturesque neighborhood on the European side of Istanbul, known for its tranquil countryside atmosphere and scenic beauty. Located away from the bustling city center, it offers visitors and residents a peaceful retreat with charming local character and wonderful natural surroundings. The area is popular for day trips from Istanbul, featuring traditional architecture, waterfront views, and access to nearby attractions. It's an ideal destination for those seeking to experience Istanbul's quieter, more authentic neighborhoods while remaining within easy reach of the city's main attractions.
