The Parthenon is the most iconic structure on the [[Acropolis]], featuring prominently in travel guides and photographs worldwide. This ancient Greek temple stands as the symbol of ancient Greek civilization and is central to [[Athens]]' identity as a historical and cultural destination. The structure is visible from multiple vantage points throughout the city, including from outdoor cafes and [[Filopappou Hill]], making it one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world.

Built in the 5th century BCE, the Parthenon showcases exceptional ancient Greek architecture and remains the finest example of Classical Greek design. Visitors can explore the temple's intricate sculptural details and learn about its significance in understanding ancient Greek culture and religious practices.
