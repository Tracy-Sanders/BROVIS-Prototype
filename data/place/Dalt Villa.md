Dalt Villa is Ibiza Town's historic old quarter and a UNESCO World Heritage site (verified 2026-04-16), representing the cultural and historical heart of [[Ibiza]]. The medieval architecture and local heritage within this UNESCO-listed old town make it one of the best things to do in Ibiza, combining centuries of history with authentic local culture. Wandering through the narrow streets offers visitors an immersive experience of the island's rich past and architectural significance.
