La Castellana is an upscale neighborhood in eastern [[Caracas]] that forms part of the [[Los Altos]] ('the heights') area. The district features modern shopping malls, trendy boutiques, and a diverse dining scene ranging from casual to fine dining establishments. As one of the safer neighborhoods in the city, La Castellana is considered more accessible for tourists to explore on foot compared to other areas of Caracas.

The neighborhood is well-connected to nearby upscale districts like [[Las Mercedes]], [[Los Palos Grandes]], and [[Altamira]], making it a hub for shopping and entertainment in the eastern part of the capital.
