Karon Beach is one of [[Phuket]]'s most well-known beaches, celebrated for its white-gold sand and jade-colored tropical waters. The beach is prominently featured in the scenic [[Karon View Point]] (Three Beaches Viewpoint) alongside [[Kata Beach]] and [[Kata Noi Beach]], offering visitors a stunning panorama of Phuket's signature coastal landscape. The waters are rich with tropical fish, making it ideal for swimming and snorkeling. The beach remains a popular destination for both relaxation and water activities.
