The Extraterrestrial Highway is a scenic route stretching across Nevada's high desert landscape, connecting [[Las Vegas]] to [[Great Basin National Park]]. Known for its unique desert scenery and quirky Southwestern character, this highway offers travelers an interesting road trip experience that combines natural beauty with the region's distinctive culture. The drive features expansive vistas of sagebrush plains, distant mountain ranges, and the kind of remote, otherworldly landscape that has made the area famous for UFO lore. Perfect for those seeking scenic drives and offbeat attractions beyond the typical tourist circuit.
