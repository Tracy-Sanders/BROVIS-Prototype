Phuket Town serves as the cultural and historical heart of [[Phuket]] island, distinguished by its well-preserved Sino-Portuguese architecture with characteristic yellow buildings. The town represents the traditional side of Phuket, offering visitors insight into local culture beyond the beach resort experience. It features street food stalls and restaurants serving cuisine with diverse influences, making it essential for understanding Phuket's multicultural heritage. Best visited for culture, architecture, history, and authentic local food experiences.
