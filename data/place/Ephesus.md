Ephesus stands as one of the world's most legendary Greco-Roman archaeological sites, representing a major historical attraction for visitors exploring Turkey's ancient heritage. The epic ruins showcase millennia of civilization, from Greek settlement through Roman imperial periods, and are featured prominently in the Treasures of Türkiye tour. Located near Izmir on Turkey's Aegean coast, Ephesus is easily accessible from Istanbul and offers unparalleled insights into classical antiquity, including the famous Temple of Artemis and the Library of Celsus.
