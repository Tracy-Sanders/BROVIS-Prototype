Biscayne Bay is the waterway that separates [[Miami]] from [[Miami Beach]], serving as a focal point for the region's geography and providing scenic views across the metropolitan area. The bay is visible from landmarks like [[Bayfront Park]] and offers abundant opportunities for water activities, island escapes, and recreational boating. Its strategic location makes it central to the Miami area's appeal as a destination for both residents and visitors seeking waterfront experiences.
