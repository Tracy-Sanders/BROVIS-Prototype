Barcelona is Spain's second-largest city and a major destination featured in multi-destination Spain itineraries like Lonely Planet's 'Spanish Classics' 12-day tour. The city is renowned for its distinctive modernist architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and Mediterranean coastal setting. Barcelona is commonly paired with visits to [[Madrid]], [[Seville]], and [[Granada]] in comprehensive Spanish travel packages, making it a cornerstone of classic Spain exploration.
