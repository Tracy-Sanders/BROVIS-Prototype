Kata Noi Beach is one of [[Phuket]]'s notable beaches, visible alongside [[Kata Beach]] and [[Karon Beach]] from the scenic [[Karon View Point]] (Three Beaches Viewpoint). The beach exemplifies Phuket's white-gold sand and jade-hued waters that characterize the island's coastal attractions. Best visited for swimming, sunbathing, and capturing scenic views of the Andaman Sea.
