Cala d'en Serra is a secluded beach located on Ibiza's northern shore, representing one of the island's hidden cove offerings. This remote beach provides an escape from the busier tourist areas and is featured among the best beaches in Ibiza. It appeals to travelers seeking pristine natural settings and quieter beach experiences away from the nightlife scene that dominates other parts of the island.

The cove is ideal for those exploring [[Ibiza]]'s northern coastal areas and seeking peaceful swimming and sunbathing opportunities in a relatively undeveloped setting.
