The Hagia Sophia is a legendary Greco-Roman architectural masterpiece and one of [[Istanbul]]'s premier attractions. Built in the 6th century, it represents the city's rich historical and cultural heritage and stands as a testament to Byzantine engineering brilliance. The monument's massive dome and intricate interior decoration make it a must-see destination for visitors exploring the [[Old City]], offering profound insights into both architectural innovation and the region's complex religious history.
