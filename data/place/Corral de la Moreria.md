Corral de la Moreria is Madrid's premier flamenco destination, presenting authentic live flamenco performances every night of the week since 1956 (verified 2026-04-15). The venue features an on-site Michelin-starred restaurant, making it ideal for combining dinner with an immersive cultural experience. Over its seven decades, the venue has hosted notable figures including Ernest Hemingway, Pablo Picasso, Che Guevara, and Demi Moore. Located in the historic [[Las Austrias]] neighborhood, it remains the gold standard for experiencing flamenco culture in [[Madrid]].
