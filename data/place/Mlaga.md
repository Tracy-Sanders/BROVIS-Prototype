Málaga is a vibrant coastal city in Andalusia and a prime gateway to southern Spain's beaches and cultural treasures. It serves as an excellent base for exploring the region, with easy access to day-trip destinations like [[Ronda]]—described as the region's crown jewel—and the beaches of [[Costa de la Luz]]. The city is featured prominently in the 'Málaga, Seville, and the Costa de la Luz' 11-day Andalusia tour package, making it a natural starting point for travelers seeking to combine beach relaxation with cultural exploration.
