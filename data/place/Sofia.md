Sofia is Bulgaria's capital and a gateway for cross-border adventures from Istanbul, connected by an overnight train service that makes it an ideal extension for Balkan exploration. The city serves as a cultural and historical hub, offering travelers a transition point from Turkey into the Balkans with relatively easy access via rail. It's best suited for those seeking to combine Turkish and Eastern European experiences in a single journey.
