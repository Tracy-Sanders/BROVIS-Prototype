Kata Beach is one of Phuket's primary beach destinations, featuring characteristic white-gold sand and jade waters that define the island's coastal appeal. The beach is part of the scenic trio visible from [[Karon View Point]], making it a central attraction for beach-focused visitors seeking swimming and scenic views. Its combination of natural beauty and accessibility has established it as a major draw for both tourists and locals exploring Phuket's southern coastline.
