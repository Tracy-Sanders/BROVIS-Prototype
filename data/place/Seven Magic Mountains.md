Seven Magic Mountains is a vibrant public art installation featuring stacked rocks painted in bold, contrasting colors located in the desert near [[Las Vegas]], [[Nevada]]. The installation has become a popular photography destination and day-trip attraction for visitors exploring the natural landscape surrounding the city. The colorful rock formations create striking visual contrasts against the desert backdrop, making it ideal for photography and art enthusiasts. It's an accessible and unique way to experience contemporary art in the Nevada wilderness.
