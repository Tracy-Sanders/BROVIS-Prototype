Orlando is a major inland Florida city renowned for its world-class theme parks and entertainment attractions. Located approximately 85 miles northwest of Miami, it serves as a premier destination for tourists and families seeking amusement park experiences. A new express rail line is currently being developed to connect Orlando and Miami in approximately three hours (verified 2026-04-16), significantly improving transportation and economic connectivity between the two major Florida cities. The rail project will enhance regional travel and strengthen Orlando's position as a key hub within Florida's tourism and entertainment landscape.
