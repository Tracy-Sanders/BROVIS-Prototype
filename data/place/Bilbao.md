Bilbao is the largest city in Spain's Basque Country and a major cultural hub in northern Spain. Featured prominently in the 'Basque Country at Its Best' 8-day tour package, it pairs seamlessly with [[San Sebastián]] and the [[Rioja]] wine region for comprehensive exploration of the distinct Basque region. The city is renowned for its striking architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and strong regional identity that sets it apart from the rest of Spain. Bilbao serves as an essential gateway to understanding Basque culture and traditions.
