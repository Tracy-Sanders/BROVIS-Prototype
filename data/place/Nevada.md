Nevada is a state in the American Southwest known for its dramatic desert terrain and striking red-rock canyons. It contains [[Las Vegas]], the region's premier entertainment destination, alongside notable natural areas including [[Great Basin National Park]]. The state's arid landscapes and vast open spaces make it a hub for adventure tourism, outdoor recreation, and desert exploration. Visitors come for world-class casinos and nightlife as well as some of the most spectacular natural scenery in the American West.
