Ronda is Málaga's crown jewel, a stunning clifftop city perched dramatically above the surrounding landscape in Andalusia. Known for its breathtaking views, well-preserved historic architecture, and romantic atmosphere, it serves as an ideal day trip destination from the coast. The town's iconic bridges, narrow winding streets, and whitewashed buildings showcase classic Andalusian charm while offering some of southern Spain's most spectacular vistas.
