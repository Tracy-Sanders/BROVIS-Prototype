Munich is Germany's most expensive city but offers diverse attractions from sprawling parks and world-class museums to historic beer halls and cutting-edge technology. The city is famous for [[Oktoberfest]], traditional Lederhosen culture, and the high-tech automotive industry (BMW). Key attractions include [[Marienplatz]], the [[BMW Museum]], [[Nymphenburg Palace]], and the sprawling [[English Garden]].

Munich serves as an ideal base for exploring Bavarian mountains, fairy-tale castles, and surrounding regions like [[Berchtesgaden]]. The city excels in beer culture, museums, parks, history, architecture, festivals, nightlife, and cultural experiences—making it one of Europe's most compelling destinations for travelers seeking both tradition and modernity.
