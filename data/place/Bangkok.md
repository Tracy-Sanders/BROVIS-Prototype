Bangkok is Thailand's capital and largest city, serving as a major hub in comprehensive Thailand travel itineraries. The city is renowned for its ornate shrines, vibrant street life, and world-class cuisine, making it a cultural and urban centerpiece for travelers exploring Southeast Asia. Bangkok is commonly featured in multi-day travel packages such as the 12-day 'Treasures of Thailand' itineraries that combine it with [[Chiang Mai]] and [[Phuket]], representing the cultural and urban dimension of Thai travel experiences. The city offers endless opportunities for temple exploration, street food discovery, and navigating its dynamic neighborhoods.
