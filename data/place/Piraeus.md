Piraeus is the largest Mediterranean port in terms of passenger traffic and commercial activity, functioning as Athens' primary maritime gateway. While most visitors treat it as a transit point en route to other destinations, the port itself offers attractions and serves as a major departure hub for island cruises and day trips throughout the Aegean. As a critical center for ferries and commercial shipping in the region, Piraeus remains essential infrastructure for both tourism and regional trade.
