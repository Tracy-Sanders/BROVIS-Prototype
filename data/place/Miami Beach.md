Miami Beach is a separate incorporated city located across Biscayne Bay from Miami, renowned for its beautiful sandy beaches and well-preserved Art Deco historic district. The city serves as a major tourist destination, featuring luxury hotels in the Mid-Beach area and upscale shopping and dining venues. Visitors come for the pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and iconic 1920s-1940s architecture that defines much of the beachfront. As a distinct municipality from Miami proper, Miami Beach maintains its own character while remaining a key part of the greater Miami metropolitan area.
