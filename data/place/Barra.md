Barra is a contemporary neighborhood in [[Rio de Janeiro]] known for its modern development, beach access, and sports facilities. Connected to [[Ipanema]] and [[Copacabana]] via the Ciclovia Tim Maia—a scenic bike path built as part of the 2016 Olympic Games legacy—Barra represents Rio's newer urban expansion. The area offers excellent beaches, modern amenities, and is ideal for travelers seeking a less crowded alternative to the city's more famous coastal neighborhoods while maintaining easy access to them.
