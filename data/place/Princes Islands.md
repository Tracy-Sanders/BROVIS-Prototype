The Princes' Islands are among the best day trips from Istanbul, with Büyükada being the most prominent. These islands offer wonderful countryside experiences and charming coastal scenery, providing a peaceful retreat from the vibrant city. The archipelago is easily accessible by ferry from Istanbul and features beautiful beaches, pine forests, and traditional Ottoman architecture that make them ideal for a relaxing getaway.
