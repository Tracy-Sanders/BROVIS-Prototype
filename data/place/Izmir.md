Izmir is a vibrant coastal city on Turkey's Aegean coast and a key hub in the Treasures of Türkiye tour. As a gateway to some of the region's most significant ancient sites, including the nearby ruins of [[Ephesus]], Izmir serves as an essential base for exploring western Turkey's rich historical and cultural heritage. The city blends modern Mediterranean charm with layers of history, making it an important cultural center in the country (verified 2026-04-16).

The city is well-positioned for exploring nearby attractions such as [[Cunda Island]] and other Aegean destinations, while also serving as a convenient starting point for journeys to [[Cappadocia]] and other major Turkish landmarks. Its waterfront promenade and local markets reflect the cosmopolitan character that has defined Izmir throughout its long history as a major trading port.
