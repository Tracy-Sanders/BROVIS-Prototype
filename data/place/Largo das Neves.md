Largo das Neves is a charming historic plaza located in Rio de Janeiro's Santa Teresa neighborhood, celebrated for its traditional cafes and vibrant cultural atmosphere. The square exemplifies the bohemian character and architectural heritage that define Santa Teresa, making it an ideal destination for visitors seeking an authentic local experience. The area is particularly popular for its cafes, where you can enjoy Brazilian coffee and pastries while soaking in the neighborhood's artistic and historical ambiance.
