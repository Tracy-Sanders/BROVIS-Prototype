Vouliagmeni Beach is one of the best beaches near [[Athens]], featuring sandy shores and excellent facilities for water activities. Located just south of the city, it's a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking coastal recreation without traveling far from the capital. The beach offers swimming, water sports, and beachside dining options, making it ideal for a day trip from Athens.
