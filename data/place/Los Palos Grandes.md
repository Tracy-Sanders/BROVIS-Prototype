Los Palos Grandes is an upscale eastern neighborhood of Caracas that forms part of the Los Altos ('the heights') area. It features modern malls, trendy shops, and excellent restaurants, making it a premier destination for shopping and dining in the capital. As one of the safer areas in Caracas, Los Palos Grandes is considered more comfortable for tourists to explore on foot compared to other neighborhoods. The area is well-developed with contemporary amenities and is popular among both locals and visitors seeking upscale urban experiences.
