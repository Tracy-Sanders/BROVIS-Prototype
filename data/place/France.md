France is a major Western European destination featuring diverse attractions from the cultural capital of [[Paris]] to the sun-soaked [[French Riviera]] and [[Provence]]. The country is celebrated for world-class cultural institutions like the Louvre, exceptional vineyards producing renowned wines, and architectural landmarks spanning from medieval cathedrals to Renaissance châteaux. Travelers explore museum collections, participate in wine tastings in Provencal vineyards, and discover art heritage through historic regions like [[Saint-Rémy-de-Provence]] and [[Arles]]. The country's combination of artistic legacy, culinary excellence, and historical significance makes it one of Europe's most visited destinations (verified 2026-04-16).
