The Old City is Istanbul's historic heart and the storied center of the city, featuring world-class attractions including the [[Hagia Sophia]] and [[Topkapi Palace]]. This region represents the rich Ottoman and Byzantine heritage that defines the cultural identity of [[Istanbul]] and [[Türkiye]]. Visitors come for exceptional history, architecture, and landmarks that span centuries of imperial rule. The district remains one of the world's most significant cultural destinations (verified 2026-04-16).
