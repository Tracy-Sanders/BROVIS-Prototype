Springs Preserve is one of Las Vegas's premier parks, showcasing Boomtown 1905—a meticulously recreated village of early Las Vegas buildings that transports visitors back to the city's founding era. The preserve combines historical education with family-friendly activities, offering interactive exhibits and outdoor spaces that explore the region's natural environment and cultural heritage. It's an ideal destination for families seeking both entertainment and learning opportunities about Southern Nevada's past.
