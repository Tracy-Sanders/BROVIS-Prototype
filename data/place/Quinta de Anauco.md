Quinta de Anauco, also known as the Museo de Arte Colonial, is a beautifully restored colonial mansion in [[Caracas]] that offers visitors a glimpse into upper-class life during [[Simón Bolívar]]'s era. The site holds profound historical significance as the place where Bolívar spent his final night in Venezuela before departing the country. The museum showcases period furnishings, artwork, and architectural details that reflect the refined tastes of colonial Venezuelan society. A must-visit for those interested in Venezuelan history, colonial architecture, and the life of South America's most celebrated liberator.
