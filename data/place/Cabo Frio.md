Cabo Frio is a popular day trip destination from Rio de Janeiro, located in [[Rio de Janeiro State]]. The city features beautiful wilderness-backed beaches and serves as an excellent option for visitors seeking smaller-scale coastal experiences and water-based activities. Known for its pristine sandy shores backed by natural vegetation, Cabo Frio offers a more relaxed alternative to Rio's bustling beaches while remaining easily accessible from the capital.

The city is ideal for swimming, diving, and other water sports, with several nearby islands and coves adding to its appeal. Its proximity to [[Rio de Janeiro]] makes it perfect for a day excursion or weekend getaway for those looking to escape the city while enjoying quality beach time.
