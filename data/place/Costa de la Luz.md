Costa de la Luz is a coastal region in southern Spain located in Andalusia, representing the beach and coastal attractions of the region. It is commonly included in multi-day tour packages such as the 'Málaga, Seville, and the Costa de la Luz' 11-day itinerary, which pairs coastal experiences with cultural city visits. The region complements the cultural attractions of nearby cities like [[Seville]] and [[Málaga]], offering travelers opportunities for beach relaxation and coastal exploration along the Atlantic coast.
