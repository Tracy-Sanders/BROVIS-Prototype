Galazia Nera refers to the saturated blue waters of the [[Cycladic Islands]] region, accessible via catamaran cruises from [[Athens]]. This scenic landmark is renowned for its vivid azure coloring and is ideal for beaches, water activities, and sailing. The waters are featured as a highlight in the 'Dreams of Greece' 12-day tour package, making it a popular destination for visitors exploring the Greek islands.
