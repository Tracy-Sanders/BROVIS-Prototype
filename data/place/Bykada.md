Büyükada is the largest island in the Princes' Islands archipelago, located just off the coast of Istanbul. Known for its beautiful coastlines, scenic views, and charming blend of Greek and Turkish cultural influences, it's one of the most popular day trip destinations from Istanbul. The island features pristine beaches, pine forests, and traditional architecture that make it an ideal escape from the bustling city.

Best visited during warmer months for swimming and beach activities, Büyükada offers both relaxation and exploration opportunities. Ferry services from Istanbul make it easily accessible for day visitors seeking coastal scenery and a slower pace of island life.
