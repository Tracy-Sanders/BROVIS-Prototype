Altamira is one of the safer and more upscale areas of Caracas, located in the eastern section as part of the Los Altos ('the heights') collective. The neighborhood offers excellent shopping, dining, and other commercial amenities where tourists can feel more comfortable exploring on foot. With its relatively secure environment and modern infrastructure, Altamira serves as a preferred base for visitors seeking a more comfortable urban experience in the capital.
