The Acropolis is one of Athens' most celebrated attractions, featuring the relics and ruins of ancient Greece including the iconic [[Parthenon]] temple. The site is undergoing accessibility improvements with a new lift and updated walkways to accommodate visitors with mobility impairments (verified 2026-04-16). Recently, visitor limits have been imposed to protect the ancient site from overcrowding (verified 2026-04-16). The Acropolis can be viewed from various vantage points including [[Filopappou Hill]].
