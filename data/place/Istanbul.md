Istanbul is a vibrant city spanning Europe and Asia with more top-notch attractions than minarets. The historic Old City and Bazaar District anchor the European side, while neighborhoods like Beyoğlu and Kadıköy offer contemporary culture and nightlife. The city is famous for the Hagia Sophia, Topkapi Palace, and Grand Bazaar—essential stops for any visitor—plus traditional Turkish experiences including coffee culture, börek pastries, and hammams.

The city's compact centers feature excellent public transportation, numerous free attractions including mosques and markets, and shopping ranging from high-end boutiques to locally crafted goods. Whether exploring the Golden Horn's waterfront, wandering through centuries-old bazaars, or sampling street food, Istanbul delivers world-class experiences across history, architecture, culture, and cuisine.
