The Battle of Carabobo was a pivotal military engagement in Venezuela's struggle for independence from Spain. This historic battle is commemorated in a monumental ceiling mural at the [[Capitolio Nacional]] in [[Caracas]], reflecting its profound importance to the nation's independence movement. The battle site and its artistic representation serve as important reminders of Venezuela's fight for freedom and sovereignty during the early 19th century.
