Las Mercedes is one of Caracas's safest and most upscale neighborhoods, located in the eastern section of the city as part of the Los Altos district. The area is home to most of the city's major shopping malls, trendy boutiques, and high-end restaurants, making it the primary destination for upscale tourism and dining in Venezuela's capital.

Visitors can navigate on foot more comfortably here than in other parts of Caracas, though Spanish language skills are helpful for getting around. The neighborhood offers excellent nightlife options and is generally considered more secure for tourists exploring the city.
