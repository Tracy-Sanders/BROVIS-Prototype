Syntagma is a key neighborhood in central Athens, serving as a hub for visitors exploring the city's cultural attractions and urban landscape. Located in the heart of the capital, it functions as a primary destination area for tourists alongside the nearby [[Plaka]] district. The neighborhood offers excellent access to museums, galleries, shops, and restaurants that showcase Athens' vibrant contemporary culture while remaining close to historical sites.
