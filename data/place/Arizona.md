Arizona is a Southwestern state featuring striking red-rock canyons and dramatic desert landscapes. Home to the iconic Grand Canyon, Arizona serves as a major destination for outdoor enthusiasts and day-trippers from nearby [[Las Vegas]]. The state is renowned for its natural wonders, including Sedona's crimson formations, Monument Valley, and the Sonoran Desert. Arizona offers exceptional opportunities for hiking, photography, and experiencing some of North America's most visually stunning geological formations.
