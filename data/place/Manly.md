Manly is a vibrant coastal suburb of Sydney, best reached via one of Australia's most scenic ferry journeys. The ferry ride itself is a highlight, offering stunning views of Sydney Harbour and the surrounding waterfront as it winds through the city's iconic geography. Once there, visitors enjoy excellent beaches, coastal walks, and the relaxed beachside atmosphere that makes Manly a favorite escape from the city center. The combination of natural beauty and accessibility makes it an essential Sydney experience.
