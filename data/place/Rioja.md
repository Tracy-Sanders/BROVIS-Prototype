Rioja is Spain's most celebrated wine region, located in northern Spain and often featured in Basque Country travel itineraries. The region is world-renowned for its exceptional vineyards and wine production, particularly its distinctive red wines. Rioja complements visits to nearby cultural destinations like Bilbao and San Sebastián, making it a key stop on the 'Basque Country at Its Best' 8-day tour package that combines Basque cultural and culinary experiences with wine country exploration.
