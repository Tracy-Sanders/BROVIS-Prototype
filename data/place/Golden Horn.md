The Golden Horn is a major waterway in Istanbul that serves as a defining geographical feature of the city. This natural inlet provides stunning panoramic views of the historic cityscape and is visible from iconic landmarks like the [[Galata Tower]]. The waterfront is filled with seagulls and offers excellent opportunities for scenic walks and photography, particularly from the [[Beyoğlu]] district. The Golden Horn remains one of Istanbul's most picturesque destinations for experiencing the city's maritime character and architectural heritage.
