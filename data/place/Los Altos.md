Los Altos ('the heights') is the collective name for the eastern section of Caracas, encompassing neighborhoods such as [[Las Mercedes]], [[Los Palos Grandes]], [[La Castellana]], and [[Altamira]]. This area contains most of the city's malls, trendy shops, and restaurants, making it the premier destination for shopping and dining in [[Caracas]]. Los Altos is considered the safest area for tourists to explore, with upscale amenities and a vibrant commercial scene that reflects the city's more affluent character.
