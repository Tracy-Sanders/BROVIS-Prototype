The French Riviera (Côte d'Azur) is a renowned coastal region in southeastern [[France]] celebrated for its stunning Mediterranean beaches, dramatic scenery, and vibrant cultural heritage. This glamorous destination has long attracted visitors seeking authentic French experiences, from charming seaside villages to upscale resort towns. The region combines natural beauty with cultural significance, offering everything from pristine beaches and scenic coastal drives to world-class museums, galleries, and Michelin-starred dining.

The area is particularly known for its luxury accommodations, exclusive beach clubs, and the famous Promenade des Anglais in Nice. The French Riviera remains a premier destination for travelers interested in combining relaxation, culture, and Mediterranean charm (verified 2026-04-16).
