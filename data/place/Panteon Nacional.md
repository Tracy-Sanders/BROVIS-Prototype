The Panteon Nacional is Venezuela's most revered national monument, serving as the final resting place of Simon Bolivar and other celebrated Venezuelan heroes. The site features stunning opulent interior design that reflects the nation's deep reverence for its independence leader. Visitors must observe strict protocols including proper dress codes (no shorts or sleeveless shirts) and maintaining respectful silence—eating, drinking, loud music, and laughter are discouraged by vigilant guards. This is essential cultural and historical site for understanding Venezuela's national identity and Bolivar's towering legacy in the country.
