The Royal Palace of Madrid is among the largest and most opulent palaces in Europe, featuring more than 1.45 million square feet of space across 3,418 rooms. The palace showcases masterpieces by renowned artists including Francisco Goya and Diego Velazquez, and houses the world's only complete Stradivarius string quintet. Visitors can witness the changing of the Royal Guard on Wednesdays and Saturdays, and explore the lavishly decorated chambers that reflect centuries of Spanish royal history and architectural grandeur.
