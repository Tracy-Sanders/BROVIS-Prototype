Şile is a charming coastal town on the Black Sea coastline, located approximately 50 kilometers northeast of Istanbul. Known for its sandy beaches and relaxed atmosphere, it serves as one of the most accessible beach destinations for day trips from Istanbul, making it popular with locals and visitors seeking respite from the city. The town offers a pleasant seaside experience with traditional Turkish hospitality, fresh seafood restaurants, and opportunities for swimming and water activities along the Black Sea shore.
