Chocolateria San Gines is one of Madrid's most iconic dessert destinations, located just a short walk from Puerta del Sol. Founded in 1864, this intimate cafe originally developed its reputation as an after-theater spot tucked behind one of Madrid's oldest churches. It remains open around the clock, serving its signature dark hot chocolate paired with freshly fried churros—a quintessential Madrid experience that has made it a must-visit for both locals and travelers seeking authentic Spanish culture and cuisine.
