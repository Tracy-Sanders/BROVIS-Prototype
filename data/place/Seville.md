Seville is the vibrant heart of Andalusia, celebrated for its world-class flamenco culture and exceptional tapas dining scene. The city features prominently in major Spain travel packages, including the 'Spanish Classics' tour and 'Málaga, Seville, and the Costa de la Luz' itinerary. Visitors should pair passionate flamenco performances with tapas tours for an quintessentially Sevillian experience. With its rich cultural heritage and lively atmosphere, Seville remains one of Spain's most captivating destinations.
