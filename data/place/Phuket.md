Phuket (ภูเก็ต) is Thailand's dazzling largest island, renowned for its white-gold beaches, jade-hued waters teeming with tropical fish, and diverse attractions. The island offers a compelling blend of natural beauty with limestone cliffs, rainforests, and cultural experiences including Phuketian heritage sites. Visitors can explore pineapple, rubber, and coconut groves, enjoy fresh seafood at beachfront restaurants, and experience both bustling tourist centers like [[Phuket Town]] and quieter fishing villages. The island serves as an ideal gateway to broader Southeast Asian adventures with easy connections to [[Krabi]], [[Khao Sok National Park]], and other regional destinations, making it perfect for beaches, island hopping, culture, food, adventure, and wildlife encounters.
