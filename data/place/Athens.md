Athens is a master of reinvention, serving up an anarchic mash-up of architectural gravitas, urban grit and infectious creativity. The city offers equal measures of grunge and grace, with attractions ranging from the relics and ruins of the [[Acropolis]] to bustling flea markets and sandy beaches. Visitors can explore ancient streets, cost-free museums, vibrant neighborhoods like [[Plaka]], and enjoy world-class cuisine. The city has tangled streets and troublesome traffic but offers numerous day trips to ancient ruins and nearby beaches.

Best for history, ancient ruins, culture, beaches, nightlife, museums, and architecture. Day trips to [[Naxos]] and the [[Cycladic Islands]] are easily accessible from the port at [[Piraeus]].
