Madrid is Spain's capital and a major hub in Spanish travel itineraries, particularly featured in the 'Spanish Classics' tour package. The city is celebrated for its famous food markets and guided street food crawls, offering visitors authentic culinary experiences alongside world-class museums and historic architecture. Madrid serves as a cultural and gastronomic gateway, often combined with visits to [[Barcelona]], [[Seville]], and [[Granada]] for comprehensive explorations of Spain's diverse regions.
