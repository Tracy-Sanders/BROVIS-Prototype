Arles is a charming historic city in Provence, France, renowned for its rich artistic and cultural heritage. The city is deeply connected to Vincent van Gogh's legacy, featuring prominently in heritage tours that trace the artist's time in the region and his influence on Provencal culture. With its well-preserved Roman architecture, vibrant art scene, and picturesque streets, Arles serves as a major cultural destination for visitors exploring the artistic traditions of southern France. The city's blend of ancient history and 19th-century artistic significance makes it essential for anyone interested in European art history and Provencal heritage.
