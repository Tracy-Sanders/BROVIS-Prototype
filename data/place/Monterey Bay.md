Monterey Bay is a stunning coastal region along California's central coast, renowned for its dramatic waterfront scenery and abundant marine wildlife. The area offers sprawling coastal trails perfect for hiking, with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and opportunities for offshore whale watching. Known for its pristine beaches, rocky coves, and rich biodiversity, Monterey Bay attracts visitors seeking both adventure and natural beauty. The region is part of the broader San Francisco Peninsula area and serves as a gateway to some of California's most iconic coastal landscapes.
