New South Wales is the Australian state that contains Sydney and its surrounding areas. The region is known for its diverse coastal landscapes, beaches, and natural attractions accessible from the capital city. Popular destinations include the iconic beaches of Sydney Harbour, the dramatic cliffs of Royal National Park, and numerous coastal towns offering excellent opportunities for swimming, surfing, and coastal exploration.
