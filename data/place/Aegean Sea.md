The Aegean Sea is a major maritime region accessible from Istanbul, serving as a primary cruise destination in Türkiye. The sea is dotted with islands like [[Cunda Island]] that blend Greek and Turkish cultural heritage, offering visitors a unique blend of Mediterranean and Anatolian influences. The region is renowned for its crystal-clear waters, historic sites, and exceptional local cuisine, making it a cornerstone of Turkish coastal tourism. From [[Istanbul]] and [[Izmir]], travelers can easily access the Aegean's islands and coastal towns for multi-day cruises and cultural exploration.
