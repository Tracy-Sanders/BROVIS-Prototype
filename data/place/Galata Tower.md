The Galata Tower is an iconic landmark visible in Istanbul's skyline, particularly striking at sunset. Located in the [[Beyoğlu]] historical area, this medieval stone tower provides scenic views of the city, the [[Golden Horn]], and surrounding neighborhoods. The tower's distinctive silhouette has made it one of the most recognizable structures in [[Istanbul]], offering visitors an excellent vantage point for photography and sightseeing. Best visited during late afternoon to capture the city's golden hour lighting.
