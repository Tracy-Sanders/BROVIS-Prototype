The Strip is the heart of Las Vegas entertainment, a legendary boulevard shimmering with neon lights and promise. This iconic stretch features the city's most famous casinos and resort hotels, earning its reputation as the perennial adult playground of the USA. Visitors can experience incredible live shows, glamorous casinos, celebrity chef restaurants, and top-tier accommodations at properties like the [[Bellagio]] with its famous fountain show and various themed resorts including the [[MGM Grand]].

Best for casinos, nightlife, entertainment, dining, shows, and luxury resort experiences.
